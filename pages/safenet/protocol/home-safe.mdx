# Home Safe

The Home Safe refers to a [Safe Smart Account](../../home/what-is-safe.mdx) on Ethereum Mainnet. It is the central hub for Safenet transactions. 
It acts as the [home chain](../chains.mdx) account for managing assets and [guarantees](../concepts/guarantee.mdx), facilitating the proper [settlement](../concepts/settlement.mdx) and validation of cross-chain transactions.
